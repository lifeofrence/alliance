About Alliance for Unity and Progress (AUP)

Driving Positive Impact

The Alliance for Unity and Progress (AUP) is dedicated to creating a positive impact in Nigeria, particularly in the northern region. Founded by Layla Ali Othman, our mission is to foster development, empower citizens, and build a more inclusive and prosperous nation.

We believe that by uniting our voices and efforts, we can overcome challenges and create equal opportunities for all. At AUP, we are committed to providing a platform for networking, creating jobs, promoting entrepreneurship, raising awareness through civic education, and providing sustainable solutions for food insecurity and healthcare.
