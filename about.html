About Alliance for Unity and Progress (AUP)

Driving Positive Impact

The Alliance for Unity and Progress (AUP) is a non-profit organization founded by Layla Ali Othman with the purpose of creating a positive impact in Nigeria, particularly in the northern region. Our mission is to foster development and empower citizens through collaboration, advocacy, and innovation.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure a United, Strong, and Self-reliant Nigeria where there are equal opportunities for all people.

Mission

At AUP, we are committed to providing a platform for networking and collaboration aimed at impacting the lives of the citizenry. We strive to create jobs, economic opportunities, and entrepreneurship while promoting awareness, civic education, and constructive media engagements. Additionally, we aim to provide sustainable solutions to address food insecurity, promote agricultural activities, create access to equal and quality education, and improve access to basic healthcare facilities and services.

Target Audience

Our programs are designed to benefit less privileged communities in Nigeria, providing them with opportunities for growth and development.

Future Plans

Our future plans include the expansion of programs, partnerships with stakeholders including the Government and Traditional Rulers, and conducting impact assessment and evaluation to ensure the effectiveness of our initiatives.

The Alliance for Unity and Progress is a beacon of hope for a better Nigeria, especially for the less privileged. We are dedicated to driving positive impact, fostering development, and creating equal opportunities across the nation. We invite you to join us in this journey and help make these dreams a reality. Together, let's build a vibrant and inclusive Alliance that will ensure every citizen has access to equal opportunities and a high quality of life.

What we are doing

We Are on a Mission to Empower the Vulnerable

Join us in our mission to create a positive impact in Nigeria, particularly in the less privileged communities of the northern region. At Alliance for Unity and Progress (AUP), we believe in fostering development, promoting education, and improving healthcare for all. Together, we can build a stronger and more inclusive nation.

Empowering individuals with the skills and knowledge to start their own businesses and create economic opportunities.

Agriculture Initiatives

Promoting sustainable agricultural practices and providing support to farmers for food security and economic growth.

Education Projects

Ensuring access to quality education for all, creating a brighter future for the next generation.

Healthcare Programs

Improving access to basic healthcare services and facilities, promoting better health outcomes for communities.
